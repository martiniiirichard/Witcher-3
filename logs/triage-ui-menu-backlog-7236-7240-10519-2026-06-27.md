### Witcher Lore Collection

Keep WLC in the reduced safe mode.

Current safe split:

- scripts active
- strings active
- DLC payload active
- `Mods\modWitcherLoreCollection\content\blob0.bundle` disabled as `blob0.bundle.disabled-by-codex-internal-test`

The disabled `Mods` bundle caused the no-window startup hang during isolation. Keeping scripts and strings preserves glossary/bestiary/lore behavior, while disabling the cooked world-placement bundle avoids the launch failure. The tradeoff is that some restored books/documents may not appear in their placed world locations.

### No Artificial Player Light

It complements the darker/less artificial lighting stack. Its residual risk is visual, not script-heavy: verify cutscenes, Cat potion behavior, and dark interiors during normal play.
