## Shrines of Verna

Keep the split compatibility setup:

- `DLC\dlcshrinesofverna` keeps world/content assets.
- `Mods\modshrinesofverna` keeps content/localization assets.
- `Mods\modshrinesofverna\...\vernaShrine.ws` is disabled because Time of the Sword and Axe already owns `W3VernaShrine`.
- `Mods\modshrinesofverna\...\vitalityRaise.ws` is disabled because Everlasting Shrines owns the better refreshed vitality effect.
- `Mods\modVernaShrine_Everlasting` owns the active shrine buff effect.

Current priority relationship is intentional:

- `modVernaShrine_Everlasting` priority `230`
- `modexpansionzero` priority `231`
- `modshrinesofverna` priority `233`

Lower numeric priority wins in this setup, so Everlasting wins the effect, ToSA wins the interaction class, and base Shrines keeps assets.
